Welcome to
Six Degrees of Francis Bacon
Six Degrees of Francis Bacon (SDFB) is a collaborative reconstruction of the social network of early modern Britain (1550-1700). SDFB shows who likely knew whom in the age of Elizabeth, Bacon, Shakespeare, and Hobbes.

First, the SDFB team mined the history of scholarship to estimate and visualize roughly 115,000 relationships. On this site, scholars, students, and citizen humanists are expanding, curating, and revising early modern British history.
